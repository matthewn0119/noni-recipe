📖 How to Browse

Head over to the Browse Recipes tab to explore all saved recipes. You can search by name or filter by category to find exactly what you're looking for.

✏️ How to Add a Recipe

Click Add a Recipe, fill in the details — name, ingredients, and steps — and press Save. Your recipe will appear in the collection right away!

💾 Saving Your Recipes

All recipes are saved right in your browser using local storage. They'll be here every time you visit on this device. No sign-in needed!

🗑️ Deleting a Recipe

Open any recipe card and scroll to the bottom — you'll find a Delete button if you ever need to remove a recipe from the collection.
