📖 How to Browse

Head over to the Browse Recipes tab to explore all saved recipes. Search by name, filter by category, or filter by who made it to find exactly what you're looking for.

✏️ How to Add a Recipe

Click Add a Recipe, fill in the details — name, ingredients, and steps — and press Save. Your recipe will appear in the shared collection right away!

☁️ Shared in the Cloud

All recipes are stored in a shared online database, so everyone sees the same collection no matter what device or browser they use. No sign-in needed!

💡 My Ideas

A personal scratchpad saved only on your device. Paste links, jot down dishes you want to try, and add notes — nobody else can see these. Capped at 50 ideas to keep your storage light.

✏️ Editing & Deleting

Open any recipe card to view it in full. From there you can edit any details, share a direct link with a friend, save it as a PDF, or delete it from the collection.
